Documentary wedding photography

Documentary style wedding photography captures very genuine moments occurring throughout a wedding day. It is all about telling a story in the picture. The couple doesn’t need to smile or pose for the photos instead pictures are being taken of these beautiful moments during the whole day.

Capturing a moment

Every photo taken has a story behind it. When looking at the photos 10 years later, the memory of these moments are still very vivid, because of the emotion, humor and reality expressed in the photos.
